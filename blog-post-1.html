The Importance of IAM in Modern Enterprises

Identity and Access Management (IAM) ensures secure and efficient access to resources. Drawing from my experience at TCS, I’ve seen how tools like One Identity Manager can prevent unauthorized access while improving productivity. IAM is crucial for protecting sensitive data in today’s digital landscape, especially with the rise of remote work and cloud adoption.

I’m excited to explore how IAM integrates with cloud platforms in the future, ensuring scalability and security for enterprises worldwide.
